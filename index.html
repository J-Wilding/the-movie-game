The Movie Game

How to Play

  • Start by choosing a movie and an unrelated actor.
  • Then you will have the choice to choose an actor in that movie or a movie that that actor has been in.
  • Continue until you get stuck!
  • Its a great game to play with friends and family (we've played for years just going around in a circle).

You'll be amazed at your movie/actor knowledge.
